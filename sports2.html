Sports in school

We have introduced many indoors and outdoors sports in our school, few are listed down and you can find more pictures in our Galary section:

  • Table Tennis: Table tennis enhances hand-eye coordination, reflexes, and mental alertness. The fast-paced nature of the game improves quick decision-making skills and concentration, while also providing an excellent cardiovascular workout in a compact space.
  • Cricket: Playing cricket enhances hand-eye coordination, strategic thinking, and teamwork. It also promotes physical fitness, discipline, and mental focus, while fostering camaraderie and social bonds among players. The sport teaches patience, timing, and the importance of both individual skill and team collaboration.
  • Volleyball: Volleyball develops agility, balance, and explosive power while promoting team cooperation and communication. It improves vertical jumping ability, reaction time, and spatial awareness. The sport also teaches players the value of position play and coordinated team effort.
  • Badminton: Badminton improves agility, coordination, and reflexes, while enhancing cardiovascular health and overall fitness. The sport requires quick thinking and strategic shot placement, helping students develop both physical dexterity and tactical planning skills.
  • Board Games: Strategic board games like chess, carrom, and scrabble enhance cognitive abilities, problem-solving skills, and logical thinking. These games improve concentration, memory, and decision-making abilities while providing a fun way to develop social skills and sportsmanship in a more relaxed setting.
